An augmented Auction House interface, used by the goblins of Undermine.

How To Use

  1. Select your realm in the top left corner.
  2. Select a category (and subcategory) from the sidebar. Optional.
  3. Enter an item name in the search text box at the top. Optional.
  4. Press the red Search button in the top right corner.
  5. Scroll through the result list to find an item of interest.
    • Click a column header to sort by that column.
    • Notice that unavailable items show when they were last seen.
    • Hover over a line for the item's tooltip.
  6. Click a line to see detailed pricing information.
    • Observe current auction prices in the list on the right.
    • Find pricing statistics and charts in the center pane.
    • Scroll down in the center pane to see more charts, heat maps, and lists.
    • Tap/Hover on charts to see tooltips with more information.

Patron Access

Paying members on Patreon enjoy additional features:
  • See prices by item level for weapons, armor, and profession equipment.
  • Check prices by breed for battle pets.
  • Unlock all the options under the Filter button:
    • Search for items in a specific level or rarity range.
    • Find items introduced in a particular expansion.
    • Split apart, or group together, the different varieties of each item.
    • Hide out-of-stock items.
    • Include region median prices in search results.
    • Use Arbitrage Mode to search an entire region at once.
  • Search for weapons or armor with specific tertiary stats, via the category sidebar.
  • Rearrange the sections on the pricing detail page to your preferences.

How to Sign Up
